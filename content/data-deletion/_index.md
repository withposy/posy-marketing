## How to Delete Your Account and Data

If you would like to delete your Posy account and all associated personal data, please send an email to **hello@withposy.com** with the following details:

- **Subject line:** Account Deletion Request
- **Include:** The email address you used to register your Posy account

We will process your request and delete your account and all associated data. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

If you have any questions about this process, feel free to reach out to us at **hello@withposy.com**.
