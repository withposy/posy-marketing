## 5. Memory Lane / Who Am I?

<div class="blog-photo-grid">
<img src="https://i.pinimg.com/originals/f4/ce/89/f4ce898f6946c1a2dd1496c91ac08d14.jpg" alt="Memory lane bachelorette game" />
<img src="https://i.pinimg.com/originals/48/d4/52/48d452f14395bdabb5dcfa02af9bcd67.jpg" alt="Friends sharing memories at bachelorette party" />
</div>
<small>Photos via <a href="https://pinterest.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Pinterest</a></small>

Get nostalgic and messy with emotions. This one can be played two ways — go with whichever fits your vibe, or combine both for maximum feels.

**Option A — Memory Lane:** Each guest shares a funny or heartwarming memory with the bride out loud. The group votes on the best story, and the bride picks her favorite. Expect laughter, happy tears, and mild jealousy.

**Option B — Who Am I?:** Each guest writes one memory with the bride on a card (anonymously). The bride reads them aloud and tries to guess who wrote each one. It's part detective game, part sentimental moment — and the wrong guesses are always the funniest part.

<table>
<tr><th>How to play (both versions)</th></tr>
<tr><td>Each guest writes down or shares a memory with the bride (funny, embarrassing, or cute)</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Memory Lane:</strong> Share stories out loud and vote on the best one</td></tr>
<tr><td><strong>Who Am I?:</strong> Write anonymously, shuffle, and let the bride guess the author</td></tr>
</table>

<table>
<tr><th>What we love most</th></tr>
<tr><td>Perfect mix of sentimental and hilarious</td></tr>
<tr><td>Gets everyone sharing stories</td></tr>
<tr><td>The bride feels loved and celebrated</td></tr>
<tr><td>Two ways to play means it works for any group size or energy level</td></tr>
</table>

## 12. Most Likely To

The ultimate finger-pointing game. Someone reads a "Most Likely To..." prompt and on the count of three, everyone points at whoever they think fits best. The person with the most fingers pointed at them drinks (or takes a dare). It's simple, savage, and guaranteed to start arguments — the fun kind.

<table>
<tr><th>How to play</th></tr>
<tr><td>Sit in a circle and take turns reading "Most Likely To..." prompts</td></tr>
<tr><td>On the count of three, everyone points at whoever fits the prompt</td></tr>
<tr><td>The person with the most votes drinks or completes a dare</td></tr>
<tr><td>Bonus: let the bride answer first and see if the group agrees</td></tr>
</table>

<table>
<tr><th>Prompt ideas</th></tr>
<tr><td>Most likely to cry at the wedding</td></tr>
<tr><td>Most likely to lose her phone on a night out</td></tr>
<tr><td>Most likely to hook up with someone at the wedding</td></tr>
<tr><td>Most likely to give a speech that makes everyone ugly cry</td></tr>
</table>

<table>
<tr><th>What we love most</th></tr>

<tr><td>The bride's answers are always surprising</td></tr>
